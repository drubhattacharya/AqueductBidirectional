How the scoring engine works:

Each of the 10 SDOH project types has a base score across all 8 dimensions, calibrated to published evidence. When you click "Score All Projects," the engine adjusts each base score using your specific profile — CHNA priorities, payer mix, HRSA designation, ACO participation, NCQA status — and re-ranks the projects for your context. Projects that directly address your top CHNA priorities receive a boost. Projects that align with your ACO's quality withhold measures receive additional weight. The result is a ranked list specific to your organization, not a generic template.
